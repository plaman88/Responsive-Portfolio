About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. I moved to Chicago in September of 2014 with huge entrepreneurial ambitions. When I first moved to the city I worked for a local tech startup. I wanted to be a part of the startup community and this was my foot in the door. Shortly there after I realized there was a huge gap in my skillset. To fill this void I joined the ranks of the Northwestern Coding Bootcamp. My goal is to develope technical background for both my own projects and future career prospects as well.
